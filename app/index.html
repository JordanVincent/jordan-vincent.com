Projects

SixDoors

May 2013 to now
web, web mobile, server
sixdoors.com

I'm working as a web developer intern at SixDoors for one year. I stopped my studies at INSA of Lyon one year to follow my dreams and work for a San Francisco's Startup! SixDoors is an iOS app which allows customers to shop from local stores and been delivered the same day. I'm in charge of the development of the Sixdoors web apps:

  • The SixDoors website: the company front page.
  • The Seller web app: a web app used by sellers to list products, and manage their account and orders. It was first developed with EmberJS and then I switched to AngularJS.
  • The Courier web app: an app developed with Sencha Touch 2 and used by the SixDoors couriers to deliver the products as fast as possible. It runs on every recent device.
  • The SixDoors Shopify app: it integrates Shopify with SixDoors. It allows stores to connect their Shopify account to SixDoors and automatically synchronize their inventory.

That's the first time I take part to this kind of project. All had to be developed from zero and in a very short time. I had to learn two new Javascript frameworks: EmberJS and AngularJS, a new syntax: CoffeeScript, a new language: Rails and to deal with various APIs. It was very challenging !

Veritabs

February 2013 to now
web, chrome extension
Chrome Web Store

Veritabs is a Chrome extension. In Chrome, when you have too much tabs opened at a time, you can't see the tabs title or even the icon. With Veritabs, the tabs are displayed vertically, so can allway see the name. It's easy to organize them, delete and create new tabs. I will gradually add new features like grouping or saving tabs.

I developed this small extension in JavaScript using the Google Chrome API.

The app has been reviewed by many tech websites like cnet.com, pcworld.com or addictivetips.com.

City Domination

Sept. 2011 to Dec. 2012 (1 year 4 months)
web, web mobile
citydomination.com

City Domination is mobile geolocated game. Use your smartphone trying to conquer the largest part of the city, the real one. In fact, the whole city is divided in zones which correspond to real buildings: your university, the city hall and even your house. But, it's impossible to win alone, that's why you must cooperate with your gang members. The social part is a big plus as people can play with their friends and neighbors. The game is inspired by the SOLOMO concept which combines social connections in a local perimeter using mobile phones.

I'm one of the four founders. I participated in all the project phases, from the idea, to the launching. I was responsible for the gameplay, the graphics and all the front-end part, that is to say the website and the webapp. I used the Javascript framework Sencha Touch 2 to quickly build a maintainable iPhone webapp. Moreover, I helped to build the server part which is based on the PHP framework Symfony 2. We used a REST architecture and developed our own API. It was very useful to communicate easily with multiple apps: website, webapp, Android app.
I left the project in December 2012 due to a lack of time and my studies at INSA of Lyon.

Smart Bricks

Sept. 2011 to May 2012 (9 months)
web
smart-bricks.com

Smart Bricks is a web store selling original LEGO sets. I realized that there is a large LEGO adult community. They spend their time building nice models, creating a myriad of blogs and organizing events all over the world. Some of them have a real talent in building with LEGO bricks and their creations are envied by fans. I contacted artists in order to duplicate and sell their creations. I've an agreement with a Spanish artist. Now LEGO fans can now order a nice 2000 pieces LEGO house on my website. The product is delivered with all the pieces, and a booklet containing the building instructions in a nice cardboard, like official LEGO sets.

LEGO artists had nothing to do, I took care of everything: creating the building instructions in 3D, designing and printing the box and the booklet, buying the pieces, shipping the model... It was very complex and time consuming however I'm proud of myself because I realized the project, from the start to the end.
